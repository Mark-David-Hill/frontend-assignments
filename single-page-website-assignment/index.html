Watership Down

By Richard Adams

Introduction

Watership Down is a novel by Richard Adams which was originally published in 1972. It's set in Hampshire in southern England and follows the adventures of a group of rabbits who flee from their home and are forced to search for a new one while learning to work together to survive. It was Richard Adams's first novel, won several awards, and was later adapted into films and tv series
