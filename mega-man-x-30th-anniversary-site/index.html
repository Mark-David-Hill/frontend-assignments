Mega Man X 30th Anniversary Fan Site

Introduction

Mega Man X was an action-platformer game by Capcom. It was originally released for the Super Nintendo and Super Famicom in December 1993. It was a sequel series to the Mega Man games that were released on the NES, and featured new characters, gameplay mechanics, and upgrades.

Features

  • 16-bit graphics
  • New storyline and charactesr
  • New dash and wall-jump mechanics
  • A variety of new upgrades, including armor pieces that could be found hidden in the stages
  • Stages that dynamically change based on what stages have already been completed
