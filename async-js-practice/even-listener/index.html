Pizza Toppings

Toppings Generally Accepted:

  • Pepperoni
  • Sausage
  • Mushrooms
  • Pineapple

Toppings Generally Not Accepted:

  • Rhubarb
  • Oranges
  • Cereal
  • Milk

There is a rumor that some people don't like pineapple on pizza. The reality is that you don't choose pinapple. Rather, pineapple chooses you.
